About us

WE LIVE BY THE CODE OF PASSION

Meet BATUMBA!

BATUMBA IS MORE THAN A CONCRETE DUDE; HE’S AN EMBODIMENT OF OUR TEAM SPIRIT AND A RESULT OF TEAMWORK

HE’S TWO METERS TALL
WE DARE YOU TO CLICK ON HIM!
BATUMBA IS THE MASCOT OF LAZAREV. AGENCY AND OUR LUCKY CHARM. HE STAYS AT OUR OFFICE TO THIS DAY AND REMAINS AN INDISPENSABLE PART OF OUR DESIGN COMMUNITY.

Back in May 2021, we commemorated our sixth birthday and developed this creature for the sake of unity. Everyone put their souls into creating this masterpiece with their unique input and imaginations.

our story

Back in 2015, we understood our passion for digital products and decided to bring even more to this world.

That’s how it all started. Six years in the making, we are a product agency of over 50+ employees now. Our goal is to create products that users love.

OUR CLIENTS LOVE US FOR BEING CORDIAL BUT ALSO FOR BEING THE BEST AT WHAT WE DO — USER RESEARCH, PROTOTYPING, INTERFACE DESIGN, AND MUCH MORE.

principles

passion

is our craft

/00-1

We all know it's impossible to take joy in something you don't believe is your calling. That's why every one of us enjoys what we do. With that, no task is too big to handle.

sincerity

/00-2

We work transparently and value people who are honest, straightforward, or even blunt. It's crucial for us to listen to our employees and clients. So, we ask, listen, and act right away instead of lingering in uncertainty.

diligence

/00-3

Each project is an adventure for us. So we drill down into the details and create products that we are proud of – products that bring value to users and profits to the business.

Humor

& Irony

/00-4

We survive all the calamities of life by having fun. We stock up on good spirits and overcome all troubles. There's no place for boredom or seclusion within the agency. Instead, we cultivate an open and fun environment.

Amiable

Nerdiness

/00-5

We're friendly nerds who scrupulously explore every detail to get to the real root of the matter; this is our default approach to work. Being nerds, we leave no room for boredom but rather treat things with kindness and smiling eyes. 

Jerks

Shall Not Pass

/00-6

Once in a while, we encounter people we don't trust. Since we don't dabble in deceitfulness, schemes, or any other kind of lies, we filter such people, both in clients and potential coworkers. Life is too short to work with good-for-nothing jerks.
